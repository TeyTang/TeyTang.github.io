Tey Tang

        Hi, my name is Tey Tang. I am an independent game developer. I graduated with a degree in computer science from UNCG in 2019. I started game development in 2020 after many months of struggling to find work. My passion for gaming led me to develop my first mobile game called “birdy outbreak” in about two weeks. Ever since, I have been developing mobile games independently. So far, all of my games are only available in the Apple App store. I hope to expand that to the Google Play store soon using multi platform development tools such as Flutter and Unity. I have a Facebook page for my game development project called “Mobile Games By Tey” for those who would like to keep up with my  projects. However, mobile game development is not my only skill, I also create apps and make tutorials on how to make apps/games on youtube. My experiences range from Windows software development to app development using tools such as Android studio, Xcode, flutter, Google Firebase Firestore, Google Firebase Storage. I also have expereince making website using PHP, HTML, and MySQL combination. Click here to follow me on Facebook.
